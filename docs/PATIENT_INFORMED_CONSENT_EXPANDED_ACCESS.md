## 2. DOSING PROTOCOL & TEMPORAL BOUNDARIES
*   **Daily Dose:** You will take exactly one (1) tablet every 12 hours (q12h) with 250mL of water.
*   **Maximum Ceiling:** Under no circumstances will this treatment exceed twenty-one (21) consecutive days.
*   **Mandatory Termination:** On Day 21, you must immediately stop taking the tablets and enter a 14-day mandatory drug-free washout period.

## 3. IDENTIFIED HEALTH RISKS & CONTRAINDICATIONS
*   **Liver Strain:** The active components are processed by the liver. Extended use or high doses can cause drug-induced liver injury (DILI) and drain natural cellular antioxidants (glutathione).
*   **Pediatric Exclusion:** This drug must never be administered to children or infants due to high risks of developing chemical encephalopathy (Reye’s-like Syndrome).
*   **Pre-existing Conditions:** You are excluded from this protocol if you have a history of gallstones, liver cirrhosis, or elevated liver enzymes (AST/ALT).

## 4. SANITATION & PUBLIC HEALTH CONTAINMENT CONTROLS

*   **Wastewater Protocols:** To prevent environmental contamination by persistent active plant residues, you must drop a concentrated sodium hypochlorite (Clorox/bleach) puck into your upper toilet tank before beginning the protocol. You must flush once *immediately before* using the bathroom, and use the provided at-home colorimetric dye strips to track clearance on Days 22, 23, and 24.
