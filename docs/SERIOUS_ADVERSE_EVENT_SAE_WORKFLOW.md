## STEP 1: IMMEDIATE INTERVENTION (T + 0 HOURS)
1.  **Dosing Revocation:** Order the subject to halt all consumption of the *Azadirachta indica* pure matrix tablets.
2.  **Washout Switch:** Move the subject directly into the 14-day zero-exposure tracking window.
3.  **Supportive Care:** Order an immediate liver panel re-screen and administer standard oral or IV hydration to shield renal pathways from active acidic plant fractions.

## STEP 2: FEDERAL AGENCY REPORTING (T + 24 HOURS)
1.  **The Form:** Populate **Form FDA 3500A (Mandatory MedWatch Safety Reporting Document)**.
2.  **Required Parameters:** Detail the exact lot tracking numbers, total cumulative milligram exposure over the treatment block, the magnitude of transaminase or creatinine spikes, and the current triage status of the subject.
3.  **Submission:** Upload the encrypted document through the FDA Electronic Submissions Gateway (ESG).

## STEP 3: IRB AND SPONSOR NOTIFICATION (T + 48 HOURS)
1.  Deliver a formal signed copy of the completed Form FDA 3500A directly to the reviewing Institutional Review Board chair.
2.  Flag the specific clinical metric that triggered the stop-gate to verify internal compliance safety frameworks were fully implemented.
